About The Creators

Angela M. Francisco

A 17 year old illustrator who likes to draw her favorite anime characters. Her passion is drawing and her hands have been doing the artistic work since she was young. Angela is a hardworking student who aims to be a Medical Doctor in the future. She shares her illustrations to most of her friends and plans to begin accepting art commissions once she turns 18.

Kyle Mc Leandrey Rodriguez

He began writing stories and rhymes of poetic expressions when he was just 13. Now, 18 year old Kyle longs for a wider audience rather than maintaining his small career (not paid) as a Wattpad author. Kyle aims to be a Game Developer once he graduates college, and is currently inspired by gamers other than being a fan of music and literature.
