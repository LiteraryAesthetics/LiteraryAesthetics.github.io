POETRY

Saved

Painted hearts of destinies,
an art of falling for you;
remind me constantly, dear,
keep on making me feel so new.

Just beneath the seas and oceans,
islands, mountains and distance—
how beautiful could it be to
obtain someone with such apearance?

Over the glowing, thousands of stars,
no one knew you'd come to me;
I was saved from the darkest places,
for you and I were meant to be.

The painted hearts of destinies,
art of saving your heart and love;
you were the best, most beautiful thing
someone like me could ever have.

I Love You (Valentines 2019)

These three short words
have been heard a billion times;
for every day that passes
or perhaps for valentines.

I love you—once said
to convey a deep affection,
to reminisce a love story,
or for desire and infatuation.

Perhaps, my fine sweetheart,
to your wondrous eyes I utter—
I admire your splendid quiddity
for making my weak heart flutter.

Certainly, these three words
have been said by various hosts,
but perhaps I want to hear these
few words from you the most.

Altering Devotion

It is as if you crafted
my mind to have only
the thought of your eyes
the entire time.

It is as if you have fixed
my lips to speak only
your name like uttering words
is a death-dealing crime.

It is as if you molded
my heart to beat only
for you and to love like
you are utterly mine.

Monarchy

You are not a random lady— 
you'll conquer my whole castle,
for I ask you to be my woman
and leave to me the battle.

You're not just a Princess; 
you have more to expect.
Not like a game of chess,
I will be the one to protect.

You are a delicate throne
who happened to be my Queen.
I considered you my own,
so consider me your King.

We Already Met

Caught in a moment
of a perfect disaster—
we watched the lights
run with colors.

Like the breathtaking sky
of pink and orange,
or a scenario of gardens
filled with flowers.

We did not only feel
the words so plain;
it reached as if it came
from our voices.

We did not only look
at the brightness of screens;
we watched the night stars
leave us wishes.

Always

There's always a touch of truth
in every "I'm just kidding." 
For all of the jokes I told,
I made to see you laughing.

'Cause there's always a little fear
in every "I'm not scared."
For loving you itself
is a thing I've always feared.

There's always a little pain
in every "I am fine." 
For seeing you with him
always breaks this heart of mine.

Next Time

I swear, baby, next time
when our little chances come,
I would not let go forever,
and you'll be my only one.

I swear after the sunset
we will both run to the stars;
with the moon's bright glimmer
I will spend the timeless hours.

I swear, baby, tomorrow,
we will jump outside the line;
I swear, baby, next time
I'm going to make you mine.

Fur Elise

For my love, Elise,
my one and only;
the sweetness and laughter,
my best symphony.

You're not the Elise
that's been loved by composers;
you're real—my melody,
you're different from others.

You are my Elise,
but I'm the one you've broken.
I thought you were mine,
but I'm not your Beethoven.

What Love Is

Love is a song
that's sung by birds;
a story that's told
by fluttering words.
An old little piece
with a colorful tone,
but for this heart of mine,
love was still unknown.

You Are

You are that missing
piece of this heart—
I am that only artist,
you are an ethereal art.

Your tips, your body,
a seductive, luring trap;
I'm tempted yet there still
remains a long, mile gap.

I wish for you to be
happily celebrating,
for you still have me here,
to give you all my loving.

I love you, though distance
between us is really far;
you are my ever after,
you are, my dear, you are.

Poems

There really are things
can't be seen by the eyes—
like the soft wind by the fields
or a soul when a person dies.

A poem—
is something I can see,
but it also gives me things
I can feel inside of me.

It seems so amazing
to see these words and letters;
to feel it inside my heart,
to hear these cries and laughters.

A poem—to me
is something that has no end.
No limits of sights or feelings,
a deadly yet undying friend.
